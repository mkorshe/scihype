Why SciHype?

The final aim of every research is to improve people’s life. But how can we achieve it if there is no direct connection between cutting-edge scientific discoveries and people? SciHype’s goal is to bridge people with the most recent advances in the biomedical by providing a summary of the most modern observations and innovations.

We are screening and processing multiple high-impact journals and other sources to empower reader with facts and statistical outlines.
