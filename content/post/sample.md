### Why SciHype?

The final aim of every research is to improve people's life. However, there is a weak connection between cutting-edge scientific discoveries and people.

**SciHype's** goal is to bridge people with the most recent advances in the biomedical  by providing a summary of the most modern observations and innovations.

We are screening and processing multiple high-impact journals and other sources to empower reader with facts and statistical outlines.
